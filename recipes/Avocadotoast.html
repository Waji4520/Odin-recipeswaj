Avocado Toast Recipe

Description

Avocado Toast is a recipe that has taken the world by storm due to its popularity on tiktok and other social media outlets.

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Avocados or Guacamole
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Sundried Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cut bread in half
  2. Toast bread
  3. Mash avocados
  4. Spread avocado on toast
  5. Drizzle Balsamic on top
  6. Add sundried tomatoes
