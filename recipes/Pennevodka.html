Penne Alla Vodka Recipe

Description

Pasta boiled aldente and mixed with a pink cream sauce also my bubbas favorite!

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Heavy Cream
  • Basil
  • Penne Pasta
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Sautee garlic and onions in oil
  2. Add tomato sauce
  3. Add basil
  4. Add heavy cream
  5. Add pasta
