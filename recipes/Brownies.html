Brownies Recipe

Description

Dense decadent chocolate cake in brownie form my bubbas favorite

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Choclate chips
  • Brownie mix
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix brownie mix with eggs and milk
  2. Add Butter
  3. Add choclate chips
  4. Put in baking tray with oil and bake for 45 mins
